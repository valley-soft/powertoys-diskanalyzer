Our Mission

ValleySoft was founded with a singular focus: to build deeply integrated, highly performant software for the modern Windows ecosystem.

In a software landscape dominated by bloated electron apps and slow web wrappers, we believe that native code still matters. We build tools that feel like they belong natively inside the operating system—tools that launch instantly, respect your battery life, and seamlessly plug into platforms like the Windows Command Palette and PowerToys.

Our Core Principles

  • 1. Bare-Metal Performance

    We write our software in C# utilizing native Windows APIs and WinUI 3. We obsess over milliseconds, memory allocation, and multi-threading so that when you ask your computer to do something, it happens instantly.

  • 2. Absolute Privacy

    Your data is yours. Period. ValleySoft applications are designed from the ground up to operate 100% offline. We do not include telemetry, we do not phone home, and we do not track your usage. Our tools analyze your system entirely locally.

  • 3. Power User Workflows

    We build tools for users who keep their hands on the keyboard. Whether it's through the Command Palette, PowerToys Run, or advanced CLI arguments, we design interfaces that prioritize speed and efficiency above all else.

The Team

ValleySoft is driven by a passionate team of developers based in the United States, dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what native Windows development can achieve.
