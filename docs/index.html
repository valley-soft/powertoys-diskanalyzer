Drill Down

Find the exact folders eating your storage.

No more guessing why your C: drive is full. Disk Analyzer maps your entire directory structure hierarchically.

Double-click any row to instantly drill down into subdirectories, revealing massive caches, forgotten video files, and bloated dependencies.

Keyboard First

Integrated directly into your workflow.

Are you a keyboard warrior? Disk Analyzer is available as a native extension for the PowerToys Command Palette.

Hit your shortcut, type `>`, and begin navigating and analyzing your drives without ever taking your hands off the keys.

Visualize

Understand your data at a glance.

Text tables can only tell you so much. Disk Analyzer generates crisp, highly responsive charts that visualize your drive's composition.

Identify which specific applications or system folders are hoarding your capacity with absolute clarity.

Native Performance

Written in C# using native Windows APIs. We bypass bloated abstractions to scan massive physical drives and PCIe SSDs in a matter of seconds.

Absolute Privacy

Zero telemetry, zero internet connections, and zero tracking. Your file system metadata is processed entirely offline and never leaves your device.

Standard User Security

Operates cleanly without requiring Administrator privileges. Standard Microsoft Store security policies are strictly respected by default.
